Why does my summary look incomplete?

Some websites block access to their full content. When this happens, Vibe Reader can only summarize what's available.

Why this happens

  • The article is behind a paywall (paid subscription needed)
  • The website requires login
  • The website blocks external access

How to fix it

Android can't yet send articles back from the browser. For now, the easiest fix is to use the Vibe Reader Chrome extension on your desktop — articles you save there will sync back to your Android app.

  1. Install the Vibe Reader Chrome extension
  2. On your desktop, open the article in Chrome and sign in or unlock it
  3. Click the Vibe Reader icon in the toolbar → the page is sent to your account
  4. Open Vibe Reader on Android — you'll see the complete summary
