Delete Your Vibe Reader Account

If you would like to delete your Vibe Reader account and all associated personal data, please follow the steps below.

How to delete your account

Option 1: In the app

  1. Open the Vibe Reader app.
  2. Go to Settings → Account → Delete Account.
  3. Confirm your request when prompted.

Your account and data will be scheduled for deletion immediately.

What data will be deleted

When you delete your account, the following data will be permanently removed from our systems:

  • Email address and profile information
  • Saved articles, highlights, and reading history
  • AI-generated summaries and preferences
  • Subscription status and plan details

Data we may retain temporarily

For legal, security, or billing compliance purposes, certain data may be retained for a limited period after account deletion:

Data type Retention period Reason
Payment and transaction records Up to 180 days Tax and accounting obligations
Fraud and abuse prevention logs Up to 90 days Security and legal compliance

After the retention period expires, this data will be securely deleted.

Questions?

If you have any questions about account deletion or your personal data, please contact us at vibe-reader-support@seekrtech.com.
